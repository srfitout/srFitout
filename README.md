# SR Fitout — Turnkey Interior & Civil Construction Studio

`Dream it. Create it. Live in it.`

SR Fitout is a premier turnkey interior design and civil construction studio based in Frazer Town, Bengaluru. We specialize in crafting residences, commercial workspaces, modular setups, and civil modifications with precision, care, and a single point of accountability.

## ✨ Our Core Services

At SR Fitout, we offer comprehensive turnkey solutions across four major disciplines:

### 1. Civil Work
High-precision structural and groundwork for residential and commercial requirements.
* **Masonry & Partitioning**: Robust brickwork and layout adjustments.
* **Flooring & Plastering**: Flawless surface leveling and installation.
* **Structural Modifications**: Safe modifications, remodeling, and structural enhancements.
* **Waterproofing & Tiling**: Professional moisture control and premium floor/wall tiling.

### 2. Commercial Interiors
Productive, brand-aligned workspaces designed to maximize efficiency and flow.
* **Office Layouts**: Ergonomic workstations, executive suites, and conference rooms.
* **Retail Showrooms**: Eye-catching product displays and retail customer routes.
* **Collaborative Spaces**: Modern breakout zones, lounges, and client greeting spaces.

### 3. Modular Interiors
Factory-finished modular installations built for speed, durability, and a clean fit.
* **Modular Kitchens**: Smart space optimization across L-shaped, U-shaped, and parallel kitchens.
* **Wardrobes & Storage**: Sliding, openable, and walk-in wardrobes with custom configurations.
* **Smart Storage**: Custom storage modules customized for modern living.

### 4. Residential Interiors
Personalized living environments crafted around your lifestyle.
* **Living & Dining Areas**: Warm, inviting focal zones for family and guests.
* **Bedrooms**: Cozy master suites, guest rooms, and functional kids' bedrooms.
* **Lighting & Ceilings**: Contemporary false ceiling designs and architectural lighting.

## 📋 Our 5-Step Workflow

We guide you from initial concept to the day you move in:

1. **Consult**: Client brief, initial site visit, and feasibility analysis.
2. **Design**: Customized floor layouts, 3D renderings, and material palettes.
3. **Estimate**: Comprehensive, itemized BOQ and delivery schedules.
